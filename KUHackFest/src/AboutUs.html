About Us

We are a bunch of students trying to bring change in our society with the ideas that are brewing in our minds. This website is basically made for donating money to the womens who do not have access to sanitary pads.

Menstrual Hygiene is every women's right. Team Excess 3, was moved deeply looking at the plight of women who couldn't afford a packet of sanity pads. If she can bring precious life to this world, can we help and make her life a little easier? We thought of bringing a small change- Sponsor a packet of pads and bring a smile on her face. Our vision is simple, every women deserves free menstrual hygiene products. If we come together, to participate in this revolution maybe we can actually change the World.
